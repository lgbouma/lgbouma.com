Welcome! I am a doctoral student in Princeton’s Department of Astrophysical Sciences.

Most of my research is in exoplanetary and stellar astrophysics. I am especially interested in observing and understanding the births, lives, and deaths of planetary systems. I do this using telescopes in space and on the ground.
